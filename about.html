About

Who we are

Tabserve is an independent app studio founded by Yunus Güneş in Türkiye. We build simple, genuinely useful mobile apps and share practical, free guides on travel, trips and rentals.

Our apps

We currently build and maintain three apps, free to start on iOS and Android:

  • OneBag — smart packing lists and carry-on baggage rules to help you travel light.
  • Routevia — discover Türkiye city by city and plan your route in seconds with AI.
  • RentFlow — practical tools and calculators for landlords and rental management.

What we publish here

On this site we share original, carefully written guides — packing tips, destination guides and landlord advice — to help readers and to introduce our apps. All articles are written to be useful first.

Publisher

This site is published by Tabserve, an independent app studio based in Türkiye. Tabserve develops and operates OneBag (travel packing), Routevia (Türkiye travel) and RentFlow (rental management).

Editorial principles

  • Accuracy: place names, transport and practical details are reviewed before publishing; instead of volatile prices or opening hours we focus on advice that stays useful.
  • Independence: guides contain no paid placements or sponsored recommendations; if a piece is ever sponsored, it will be clearly labeled.
  • Updates: articles are reviewed periodically; if you spot something outdated, tell us and we'll fix it promptly.

Contact

We'd love to hear from you — questions, corrections or partnerships. Email us at teknopattv@gmail.com. We aim to reply within 48 hours on weekdays.
