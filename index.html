Travel lighter, explore smarter, manage better

Tabserve is built around one idea: a good guide is far more useful when it connects to a tool that gets the job done. We write in-depth, honest guides on the things we care about — packing & travel, exploring Türkiye, and managing rental property — and pair each with a free app that turns the advice into action.

✈️ Packing & travel

Overpacking is the most common travel mistake. Our guides cover how to pack a backpack with the zone method, a complete packing checklist, carry-on weight limits and the mistakes most people make. When it's time to pack, OneBag builds a personalized list by destination and weighs your bag against 80+ airlines.

🇹🇷 Exploring Türkiye

From a ready-made 7-day Türkiye itinerary to deep guides on Cappadocia, Antalya, Ephesus, the best beaches and when to visit, we help you plan a real trip — not just a bucket list. Routevia then turns your picks into an AI-optimized, day-by-day route across all 81 provinces.

🏠 Landlord & rental

Being a landlord is a business. Learn how to price rent correctly, calculate rental yield, screen tenants and handle security deposits and tax deductions. RentFlow keeps rent, tenants, leases and expenses in one place, with free yield and cash-flow calculators built in.

Frequently asked questions

Are Tabserve apps free?
Yes — OneBag, Routevia and RentFlow are all free to start on the App Store and Google Play.
What is OneBag?
OneBag is a travel packing app that builds smart carry-on packing lists by destination and tracks your bag's weight against 80+ airlines' cabin and checked baggage limits — so you never overpack or pay an overweight fee. See packing guides →
What does Routevia do?
Routevia helps you discover the best places to visit across Türkiye, city by city, and plan an AI-powered trip route in seconds. Gezilecek yerler →
Who is RentFlow for?
RentFlow is for independent landlords and small real-estate investors — track rent, tenants, leases and expenses, with free calculators for rental yield, cash flow and legal rent increases across 18 countries. Landlord calculators →
What platforms are the apps on?
OneBag and Routevia are available on iOS and Android. RentFlow is on iOS, with Android coming soon.
