What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites work, remember preferences and understand how the site is used.

How we use cookies

Third-party cookies (Google AdSense)

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick/Google advertising cookie) to serve ads on this Site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this and/or other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings, and review third-party options at aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu.

Managing cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the banner on the Site. You can also delete or block cookies in your browser settings at any time; note that some features may not work without them.
