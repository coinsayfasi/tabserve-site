Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tabserve ("we", "us") handles information when you visit apps.tabserve.com.tr (the "Site"). We keep things simple and collect as little as possible.

Information we collect

This Site is a static website. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect names, emails or payment details through the Site. If you email us, we receive only the information you choose to send.

Analytics & cookies

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate traffic — such as which pages are popular and which countries visitors come from. This may set cookies or similar identifiers. The data is aggregated and is not used to personally identify you.

Advertising

We may display advertising on this Site in the future, including via Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party links

The Site links to the App Store, Google Play and our app guide pages. Those services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Our apps

OneBag, Routevia and RentFlow have their own in-app privacy notices describing any data they process on your device. This policy covers the website only.

Children

The Site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
